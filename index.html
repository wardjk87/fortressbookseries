FORTRESS

DIVINELY PROTECTED

First Book in the "Fortress Church Series"

RELEASE DATE:

Deep in the heart of the Rocky Mountains just before the start of the Tribulation period lies a Fortress made without hands. It is tucked away in an obscure conservative county in Colorado. Nestled in this county is the town of Golden Valley. It is home to Fortress, a remnant church and a community of born-again Christians of various ages sold out to their Lord Jesus Christ.

The leaders of this band of believers and the network of Disciplemaker Churches they started years before are two very close long time friends Ricky Crawford and Gary Kirkman. These two godly men have been ordained of God to prepare the church for the end times and birth a great revival in America. Both left highly successful secular careers and their homes in LA to relocate their ministry to Colorado.

Crawford and Kirkman's solid biblical teachings, books, and their emphasis on intercessory prayer, evangelism, discipleship, and end-time prophecy resonates with a growing number of dedicated Christians aware of the signs of the times. Some of which have been supernaturally led of God to become part of the community at Fortress.

Eventually the county where their ministry is based becomes ground zero for cosmic, spiritual, ferocious genetically-modified predators, called giant pitwolves, and evil forces in the midst of a mighty move of God. Fortress becomes a city of refuge and Luciferian efforts cannot tread upon this ministry or the end time revival that breaks out there becaue it resides on holy sovereign ground and is protected by Almighty God and His warrior angels.

The first book ends with revival breaking out in this remote spot in the Rocky Mountains that sends out spiritual shockwaves shaking the hearts and souls of multitudes around the globe for Christ.
